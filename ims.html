International Monster Slayers is a young adult urban fantasy book series written by Bethany Helwig. Here is a list of the books currently in the series.

  1. The Curse of Moose Lake
  2. The Bite of Winter
  3. The Ghosts of Yesteryear
  4. The Dark Whisper (a novella)
  5. The Brood of Nightmare
  6. The Lone Hunter (a novella)

There are three more books planned for the series that have yet to be published.
